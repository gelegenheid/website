Top 10 Teutons Tips for Travian

1. Protect your Clubswingers – Don’t waste them early.

2. Keep the barracks running – The more clubs you have, the better.

3. Raid often – Teutons are made for raiding.

4. Build scouts – Don't underestimate scouts.

5. Don't join an alliance in early game – Less farms available.

6. Use gold – Resource bonus / farmlists / troop evacuation.

7. Protect your Clubswingers!! – THEY ARE YOUR MAIN INCOME

8. Create farmlists – This makes farming much easier

9. If attacked do not panick – Make sure to not let them steal anything

10. Don't forget to have fun' – This will be easy as teutons
